Clubs on this page:

Tri-M Music Honor Society

Description: Tri-M is a place for people wanting to better their music engagement, be a leader in the music community, and further their passion and commitment to the arts.

Advisor: Mr. McGee

Student Contacts: Sadie Dussault (sdussault2024@sharonschools.net), Brendon Wen (bwen2025@sharonschools.net)

Meeting Days: First Thursday of each month at 7:20 am

Location: Auditorium

The Desi Club

Description: Exploration of Culture and Identity of students having ties to Indian, Pakistani, Nepalese, Sri Lankan and Kolkata culture.

Advisor: Mahesh Balan

Student Contacts: mbalan@sharonschools.net

Meeting Days: Wednesdays

Location: 2302

Sharon High School Theatre Company

Description: SHSTC is a place for students to discover and develop a love for the arts. We put on around three productions a year and welcome new members. Whether you are interested in performing, building sets, or creating lights and sound -- we are happy to find a place for you!

Advisor: Meg Dussault or Alex Narcotta

Student Contacts: Sadie Dussault (sdussault2024@sharonschools.net), Ena Bordman (ebordman2025@sharonschools.net)

Meeting Days: It varies depending on what you are interested in, it is a bigger commitment than other clubs so be prepared to maybe come 3 days a week.

Location: Auditorium or Steam Room

Pitch Please

Description: Pitch Please is a student led acapella club that sings fun acapella arrangements, and is a way for kids to participate in music and singing other than choir. We want kids to be excited about signing songs they enjoy, and performing at local performances such as the choir concerts and Theatre fundraisers.

Advisor: Mr. Wray/Mrs. Glenister when she comes back from maternity leave.

Student Contacts: Sammi Grosshandler (sgrosshandler2025@sharonschools.net), Liam Becker (lbecker2024@sharonschools.net)

Meeting Days: TBD (we want to meet and see people's availability) 1x a week

Location: Choir room (1263)

Chinese Yo-Yo Club

Description: We help club members of any skill level to learn how to use the Chinese yo-yo, or to add to their existing skills. We also prepare a routine for a performance, but club members are not required to attend a performance if they do not want to.

Advisor: Ms. Ding

Student Contacts: Alan Zhang (azhang2024@sharonschools.net)

Meeting Days: Tuesdays

Location: 1407

Radio Club

Description: Radio Club is a club mainly focused on audio production and event planning. We organize Concerts, Open Mics, Karaoke Nights, and we also help with audio during school dances, town events, and Pep Rallies.

Advisors: Mr. Tessier and Mr. Bys

Student Contacts: Patrick Blaney (pblaney2024@sharonschools.net)

Meeting Days: Thursdays

Location: 2221
