Photography Club

Description: Photography Club is for any student who wishes to explore either digital or darkroom photography! Join us to go for photo walks, print in the darkroom, critique each others work, and make friendships over an interest in image making! By joining this club, you'll have access to a community of photographers who can offer feedback and support on your work, as well as provide opportunities to collaborate on projects and share resources. Our latest project has been working with darkroom photography! We're taking photos with film cameras and developing them ourselves! Our biggest project so far was collaborating with ‘Books In Bloom’. We took photos based on a book’s theme, and sent them to florists, where they handcraft bouquets to match our photos!

Advisor: Mr. Van Beckum
